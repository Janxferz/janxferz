Plot:

In Lincoln, Rhode Island, there lives a man named Darold Wichstock, nicknamed “Darold the Doomer” for his appearance and beliefs of the end of humanity. One day Darold is tired of feeling depressed all the time and decides to take some LSD, but instead of making him feel better, it just made him hungry. Darold decides to drive to the nearest fast food place, but on the way, he crashes his car into a street lamp and falls into a coma.
